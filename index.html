Aziza
Al Mugheiry

Writer. Scientist. Dreamer.

Aziza bint Seif Mohammed Al Mugheiry is an Omani scientist currently contributing to research at UK government agency known as Science & Technology Facilities Council in Oxfordshire.


She was raised in the Emirate of Dubai, where she attended International School of Choueifat Al Sofouh and was awarded AP scholar with distinctions. After completing her schooling, she received a full sponsorship from the Government of Oman to pursue her Undergraduate degree in Physics & Astronomy at Cardiff University. Her interests lie in particle and nuclear physics, modern political philosophy, diplomacy and literature.
